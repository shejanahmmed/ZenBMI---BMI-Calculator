About ZenBMI

ZenBMI is a modern health utility designed to provide a seamless, intuitive experience for monitoring your Body Mass Index.

bolt

Real-time Insights

Experience instant calculations as you slide. Our dynamic engine updates your BMI and health category in real-time with zero lag.

history

Progress Tracking

Save your results locally and track your journey. Manage records with the ability to edit or delete entries anytime.

settings_accessibility

Unit Flexibility

Whether you prefer Metric (cm/kg) or Imperial (in/lbs), ZenBMI adapts to your preference with a simple toggle in settings.

palette

Premium Aesthetic

Designed with a sleek dark-mode interface and glassmorphism elements, ZenBMI offers a premium feel across all devices.
